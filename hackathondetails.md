# Pacifica Hackathon

#### Overview

The Pacifica Hackathon is a one month, online event designed to bring together developers, quants, and builders to create innovative applications on Pacifica’s perpetuals infrastructure. Participants can compete individually or in teams of up to five members, exploring multiple tracks ranging from trading automation to DeFi composability.

The hackathon is ideal for algorithmic traders, DeFi developers, data engineers, product builders, students, and hackathon enthusiasts seeking to leverage Pacifica’s APIs and builder tools.

#### Team Rules

* Participants can join **solo or in teams of up to 5 members**.
* Teams must **register** via the [official form](https://forms.gle/1FP2EuvZqYiP7Tiy7) and join **Discord** for communication.
* Team members are recommended to **attend the kickoff** and **demo day**.
* Teams are responsible for [submitting](https://forms.gle/zYm9ZBH1SoUE9t9o7) **their own work** by the deadline.
* Teams can **change members** only during the first half of the hackathon.
* Teams MUST use **Pacifica API and/or Builder Code** to build their projects.
* All work presented in the hackathon **must be created during the hackathon period**. Pre-built projects or prior work are not eligible.
* Submissions must reflect **work performed by the registered team members**; external code or contributions must be clearly acknowledged.

#### Judging Criteria

Submissions will be evaluated by a panel during the demo day according to the following criteria:

* **Innovation**: Novelty and creative use of Pacifica’s infrastructure.
* **Technical Execution**: Code quality, API integration, and overall development rigor.
* **User Experience**: Polish, usability, and intuitive design.
* **Potential Impact**: Likelihood that traders or users would adopt the solution.
* **Presentation**: Clarity and effectiveness of the demo.

#### Prizes and Rewards

<figure><img src="https://727612817-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FO2lcakUmUFILzrKCX989%2Fuploads%2FzKd8mtRLHZzn7gfGCQi8%2FScreenshot%202026-03-01%20at%2017.10.38.png?alt=media&#x26;token=848df313-c118-46f0-bdfe-71b58fb770a6" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The Pacifica Hackathon offers a total of $15,000 in cash rewards and 100,000 points distributed across the Grand Prize, Track Winners, and Special Awards.

The **Grand Prize - Best Overall** will be awarded to one winner, who will receive $5,000 and 30,000 points. This winner is also considered a track winner and will receive the corresponding track prize.

There are four **Track Winners**, one per track: Trading Applications & Bots, Analytics & Data, Social & Gamification, and DeFi Composability. Each track winner receives $2,000 and 14,000 points. Since the Grand Prize winner is also a track winner, one of the four track winners overlaps with the Grand Prize recipient.

Two **Special Awards** will be given for categories such as Most Innovative Use of Pacifica and Best User Experience. Each special award winner will receive $1,000 and 7,000 points. In total, the hackathon will distribute $15,000 in cash and 100,000 points across all winners.
